Words that I Learnt from the Story

Commemorative:
Meaning: Relating to the act of commemorating; serving to honour the memory of a person or event.
Example: The city unveiled a commemorative statue in honour of the veterans who served in the war.

Plaque:
Meaning: A flat, thin piece of metal, wood, or other material with an inscription or decoration, often used to commemorate a person or event.
Example: The plaque on the wall displayed the names of all the donors who contributed to the new community centre.

Significance:
Meaning: The quality of being worthy of attention; importance or meaning of something.
Example: The significance of the monument lies in its ability to educate future generations about the sacrifices made by their ancestors.

Longevity:
Meaning: Long duration of life; the length of time that something lasts or remains in existence.
Example: The architects designed the monument with materials that would ensure its longevity against the elements.
